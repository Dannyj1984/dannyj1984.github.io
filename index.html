Gin Gin Geroo

After hosting a number of parties from the "boat bar" at home, we decided that hosting is our thing. We also love the outdoors, being on the road, discovering new places and meeting new people. With the awesome restoration skills provided by Tony and his love of rum; and the skills of a childhood life growing up in a public house by Katie and her love of Gin.......Gin Gin Geroo was born. We hope you enjoy the familiarity that the well-known Gins and Rums bring. Our guest Gins and Rums may also take your eye and introduce you to new taste discoveries. The possibilities are endless! Fancy a cocktail? No problem, we can shake and stir you up a flavoursome treat to your individual taste. Gin Gin Geroo looks forward to being part of your party!
